About V.SHAI TATTOO

Founded in 2015, V.SHAI TATTOO has been at the forefront of tattoo artistry, combining traditional techniques with innovative designs to create unique and personalized tattoos for our clients.

Our team of experienced artists specializes in various styles, from traditional and neo-traditional to realism, watercolor, and geometric designs. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

At V.SHAI TATTOO, we believe that every tattoo tells a story. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision and bring it to life in a way that exceeds expectations.
